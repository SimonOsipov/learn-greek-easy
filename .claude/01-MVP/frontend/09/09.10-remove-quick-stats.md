## Rationale

**Why remove stats from Settings?**

1. **Purpose Separation**:
   - Settings = Configuration and preferences
   - Dashboard = Overview and stats
   - Mixing the two creates confusion

2. **Information Architecture**:
   - Users expect stats on Dashboard/home
   - Users expect settings to be configuration-only
   - Standard UX pattern across apps

3. **Duplication**:
   - Stats already on Dashboard
   - Redundant display wastes screen space
   - Increases maintenance burden

4. **User Experience**:
   - Settings should be quick to navigate
   - Stats are not settings
   - Cleaner interface improves usability
